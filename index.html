About

I am a wandering soul with an artistic mind. I am a dog mom with an affinity for cats as well. I am a watercolor enthusiast and an animation nerd. I get emotional over simple and beautiful things. I love to bring people together, to create, and to explore; whether that is places or minds. I am a bit clumsy and probably off-balance due to my big heart. I want to get to know people, to hear their stories, to laugh and to grow. I want to see the world, to know its faces and creatures, and to try all the foods it has to offer me. I love to live; I love to feel free.

I have a very diverse background in my photography education. I have taken a class under renowned photographer George Wilson, at Walt Disney Resorts. I have taken countless courses online under photographers such as Ben Long and Chris Orwig. I have taken Harvard Universityâ€™s own course on photography, informally, online. I have done workshops and webinars, but overall, I am self taught. I have grabbed several cameras, traveled out over the years, fumbled with the settings and learned through age old trial-and-error. While I do not have a formal degree on the subject, this is why my work is so unique and fluid. This is what has worked for me.
